Herb Aplenty

A comic novel of island chaos, shortage and temptation.

When an island runs dry, private habits turn into public weather. Rumour spreads, dignity frays, outsiders complicate matters, and a community that likes to think it knows itself begins to show its workings. Herb Aplenty is a comic novel of shortage, temptation, local friction, and the trouble people make for themselves when relief sits just out of reach.

By the middle of the second week without any usable cannabis on the island, people have begun checking containers they know perfectly well are empty. What follows is a dry, socially observant comic novel about shortage, rumour, class friction, appetite, and the way an entire community can start rearranging itself around one impossible inconvenience.
