Privacy

Privacy and site information

This site is a simple public author website. The current version is intentionally light, with no account area, no newsletter form, and no embedded advertising or social feed widgets.

Personal data: the site itself does not ask visitors to create an account or submit a form. If you choose to email the address on the contact page, your message will naturally include the personal data you decide to send.

Cookies and tracking: no analytics or advertising scripts are embedded in this version of the site. No intentional site-level tracking cookies are set by the author pages as delivered here.

Retailer links: links to third-party retailers such as Amazon UK take you away from this site. Once you leave this site, the privacy and cookie practices of the third party apply instead.

Hosting: the site is intended for GitHub Pages. As with any web host, routine technical logs may exist at the hosting layer outside the page code itself.

Changes: if analytics, forms, newsletters, or other data-collecting tools are added later, this page should be updated so it stays truthful.
